Terminology

First, you should learn the words and phrases used in the Touhou community. They'll be used often on this website, and knowing them will also help you communicate with other players.

1cc: Beating the game without using continues.

Static: Refers to patterns that are exactly the same every time.

Miss: Death

Non(spell): A boss pattern that isn't a spell card.

Safespot/Blind spot: An area where no bullets can hit you.
